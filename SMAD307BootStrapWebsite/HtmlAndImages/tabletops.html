Table Tops

Virtual table tops are something I've used extensively. To be honest I don't use them anymore because in person is infinitely better, however in a pinch it's good to know your options.

  • ★Foundry VTT- Hardest to use/set up, but by far the best one if your willing to put in the work and also 50 bucks one time purchase. Also 5etools integration is beautiful.
  • Talespire- Best looking 3d VTT. Can't say Id recommends it though. It’s expensive and not great for new players cause everyone needs to buy it.
  • ★OwlBear Rodeo- Easiest to use and it's free. Not very extensive, however. Best for new DMs.
  • Roll 20- It’s ok, sort of a middle ground of complexity between Owlbear and foundry. It’s alright but isn’t my favorite.
  • Lets Role- Surprisingly capable free VTT, The only problem, is that the UI is clunky and not too well thought out. One of the only VTT that has built in youtube/spotify support though.
  • Akenforge- For IRL games with a screen table, very good, kinda useless otherwise though.
  • Dungeon Master Engine- 3d VTT, better functionally than Talespire, but still a bit rough.
  • Role- A surprisingly good free VTT, but it's lacking in a lot of ways. Can’t say I'd recommend it, but it’s worth giving it a shot. Has a lot of player safety tools if that's your thing.
